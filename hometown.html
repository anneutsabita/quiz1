    • Welcome to Jakarta, my Hometown

    Jakarta is the capital and largest city of Indonesia, a country located in Southeast Asia. It is not only the political and administrative center of Indonesia but also serves as its economic, cultural, and social hub. Jakarta is situated on the northwestern coast of the island of Java, which is the most populous island in Indonesia. Jakarta is known for its vibrant culture, diverse population, and a mix of modern and traditional elements. The city is home to a wide range of industries, including finance, manufacturing, technology, and commerce, making it an important economic center in Southeast Asia.
